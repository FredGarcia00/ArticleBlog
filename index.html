Andrew Tate

Andrew Tate is a British former kickboxing world champion, entrepreneur, and social media personality. He was born on December 1, 1986, in Washington, Tyne and Wear, England. Tate is known for his controversial views on a range of topics, including masculinity, relationships, and mental health. He has gained a significant following on social media, particularly on YouTube and Twitter, where he shares his opinions and advice on various subjects. Tate has also founded several successful businesses, including a digital marketing agency, a webcam modeling site, and a cryptocurrency exchange. He has appeared on reality TV shows such as "Celebrity Big Brother" and "Ex on the Beach." Tate's controversial statements and actions have generated criticism and backlash from some people, while others view him as a free-thinking, independent individual who speaks his mind without fear of backlash.

Tristan Tate

Tristan Tate (born on July 15th, 1988) is an American-British internet personality, former kickboxer, and the younger brother of Andrew Tate. In 2011 Tristan Tate starred on the British reality show "Shipwrecked: The Island" which boosted his fame.
